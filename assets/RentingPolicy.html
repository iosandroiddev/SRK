  5. Booking

    The Owner shall maintain an updated calendar as to when the items are available for rent in the Listing and shall abide by the same. Depending on the Renter’s Search, SRK will display the available items for rent from plurality of Owners. The Renter is free to select any Owner depending on his requirement of available dates, rental fee and other criteria. Upon selection of a particular item, the Renter can send a request to the Owner for renting the same by sending them the number of hours, days or months for which the Renter wishes to rent the item. The Owner can accept such request on its sole discretion. Upon acceptance of such request by the Owner, both the Parties shall agree to the Renting Policy of SRK and shall enter in to a Rental Agreement. Once the Rental Agreement has been accepted by the Parties, Renter shall be liable to pay the rental fee, Service Fee, security deposit, if any and other miscellaneous charges upfront to process the rental transaction. The duration of a rental cycle shall commence at 3 p.m. on the start date and ends at 3 p.m. on the last date of the period selected by the Renter.

  6. Pricing Policy

    1. The price of each item listed on SRK is expressed in Indian Rupees. The Price includes any taxes and costs, excluding possible delivery fees and extra insurance costs (if any).

    2. With the exception of promotional offers that are explicitly outlined on SRK, fees are charged to the Owner and Renter once a Rental Agreement is executed between them (“Service Fee”). However, access, registration, Listing and use of SRK is free for its Users. The Company at its sole discretion can modify its policies on Listing and Service Fee which will be effective on all subsequent transactions. The Service Fee shall be paid by the Renter to the Company upon executing the Rental Agreement with the Owner along with the rental fee for the mentioned rental cycle and security deposit. The Company acts as an escrow agent in collecting, retaining and refunding the rental fee and security deposit for the item on rental. Upon successful completion of rental cycle and safe return of the rented item back to the Owner, the security deposit taken from the Renter shall be refunded back to the Renter and rental fee shall be transferred to the Owner deducting the Service Fee to their respective bank account as given and approved by them.

    3. Confirmation of the rental service will depend on the payment of the rental fee and security deposit by the Renter even after execution of the Rental Agreement. The Service Fee is charged by the Company in the name of and to the account of the Renter and Owner, as a percentage on the total rental amount listed in the Listing. A proof of payment of this Service Fee is always available in the User’s Account. Payment by the Renter can be made online via a credit or a debit card or net banking facility of your available bank.

    4. Users are solely liable for all the taxes, fees and commissions resulting from use of SRK and its Services. Taxes including but not limited to, sales tax, service tax and VAT might be applicable to your transaction whether you are an individual or a legal entity, or whether you are an Owner or a Renter. Users are encouraged to seek tax advice from a professional.

    5. Invoicing information, billing payment and invoice history are available online in the respective User account. Once the rental fee, Services Fee, security deposit and other miscellaneous expenses are paid by the Renter, an invoice is generated and made available to your account, which is accessible. In case of the Owner, the invoice is generated upon the successful completion of rental cycle and after deduction of Service Fee from the Rental Fee.

  8. Delivery and Pickup Policy

    1. The User shall be provided with an option to select the delivery type they want for the logistics of the items rented, from the following:

      1. Open Logistics

      2. SRK Logistics

    2. In option (a), the Owner and Renter can choose and manage their own logistics. However they are responsible for the delivery time of the project at Renter’s place before the rental cycle starts. For certain items, Renter/ Owner, as the case may be who are managing their own logistics for the delivery of items, SRK shall send a unique code to the User’s registered phone number for authentication purposes. The User shall abide by the timeline mentioned in the below paragraph in relation to the delivery of the item else shall choose SRK’s logistics in order to have a smooth transaction.

    3. The transit period will be standard based on our guidelines within the city where the Owner and Renter are residing. The item shall be picked up from the Owner’s place and delivered to the Renter on or before the rental cycle starts i.e. 3 p.m. on the first date of rental cycle and the same shall be picked form the Renter’s place on the last date of the rental cycle within anytime between 3 p.m. to 9 p.m., by SRK’s logistic partners.

  9. Cancellation Policy

    1. Cancellation by Renter: Once a booking transaction is completed along with the payment of necessary fee by the Renter to SRK, Renter is entitled to cancel such booking subject to following conditions. If the Renter cancels any accepted and completed booking:

      1. within seven (7) days prior to the start date of such rental cycle, Renter will not be charged any cancellation charges;

      2. within four (4) days prior to the start date of such rental cycle, Renter shall be charged 25% of the rental fee already paid;

      3. within two (2) days prior to the start date of such rental cycle, Renter shall be charged 50% of the rental fee already paid; and

      4. any time after the above mentioned time limit, the rental fee paid by the Renter shall not be refunded unless and until Owner makes the initiation to cancel the booking.

    2. In case the rental cycle is for more than a month, then the cancellation charges (as the% of the rental fee) as mentioned will be charged on one month rental fee only and the remaining amount shall be transferred to the Renter including the security deposit. The cancellation charges collected from the Renter will be transferred to Owner after deduction of SRK’s Service Fee.

  10. Cancellation by Owner:

    1. Once a booking transaction is completed along with the payment of necessary fee by the Renter, Owner is entitled to cancel such booking subject to following conditions. If the Owner cancels any accepted and completed booking:

      1. within seven (7) days prior to the start date of such rental cycle, Owner will not be charged any cancellation charges;

      2. within four (4) days prior to the start date of such rental cycle, Owner shall be charged Rs.250/-;

      3. within two (2) days prior to the start date of such rental cycle, Owner shall be charged Rs.500/-; and

      4. any time after the above mentioned time limit, Owner shall be charged Rs.1,000/-.

    2. In above cases, Renter shall be refunded with the full amount paid for the rental including miscellaneous expenses. Further, Renter shall also be given coupons worth the cancellation charge as mentioned above depending upon the scenario. The cancellation charges shall be collected from the Owner from his subsequent transactions through SRK. Normally, a rental booking shall not be cancelled by the Owner during the rental cycle. However, in the event of Renter reporting any issue with the item rented by the Owner, the Owner either has the option to fix the item and return or cancel the rental booking incase the item cannot be fixed. In such cases, SRK shall stop the rental cycle upon the intimation of issue by the Renter and notifies the Owner. It is at the discretion of the Owner to fix the item and restart the rental cycle or to cancel the rental booking.

    3. In the event of the Owner taking responsibility in fixing the item, the item will be delivered at the Owner’s place depending on the logistics chosen. Upon fixing the item within the stipulated timeline, SRK has to be intimated with the reason for issue and initiation of the rental cycle by the Owner. The Owner is free to record his/her feedback regarding the issue with SRK. However, rental cycle in such cases shall be started only after SRK confirming with the Renter. In case the item cannot be fixed during the rental cycle, then the Owner has the option to cancel the booking request by contacting SRK. When cancellation is opted, Owner would be paid only for the days on which the item was used and Renter shall be refunded the remaining amount of rental fee and security deposit.

    4. In the event of Owner making a claim that the issue was due to the Renter manhandling the item, the procedure as mentioned above shall be followed. However, the cost of fixing the item by the Owner shall be deducted from the security deposit paid by the Renter. A proof of bill showing the repair cost is mandatory for making such claim by Owner.

    5. The cancellation can be made through the options available online on their respective profile or by contacting the call center executives of the Company.
